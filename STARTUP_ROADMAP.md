## âš ï¸ Risks & Challenges

### 1. **Chicken-Egg Problem**
- Need workers to attract clients
- Need clients to attract workers
- **Solution**: Launch with worker focus first

### 2. **Competition**
- TaskRabbit, Thumbtack, etc.
- **Solution**: Local focus, better experience, niche categories

### 3. **Quality Control**
- Bad experiences hurt reputation
- **Solution**: Verification, insurance, guarantees

### 4. **Regulatory Issues**
- Worker classification (contractor vs employee)
- Local licensing requirements
- **Solution**: Legal counsel, compliance team

### 5. **Unit Economics**
- CAC might be high initially
- **Solution**: Focus on retention, referrals
